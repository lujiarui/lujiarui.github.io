About Me

I am a senior undergraduate student at Zhiyuan College, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), Shanghai City, China. My expected graduation time is Jun. 2021. For undergraduate study, I major in both Chemisty, Mathematics & Applied Mathematics, as well as minor in Computer Science. For the first two years, my overall GPA ranked the 1st among 70 students. (I have almost finished all my chemistry-major courses then)

In my second year at SJTU, I joined the High-performance Computing (HPC) student competition team (on behalf of SJTU), advised by Key Liao and James Lin. I have participating several international HPC competitions, such as SC19 , SC20 and so on. It was a very unforgettable journey.

I have been a research assistant of Brain-like Computing and Machine Intelligence (BCMI) lab in SJTU, advised by Prof. Yang Yang since 2018. At present, I work as a research intern with Dr. Lei Li at AI Lab - ByteDance.

Research Interest

My research interests mainly focus on AI for Drug Discovery, an interdisciplinary topic involving chemistry, machine learning, and mathematics:

  • Representation learning for molecular modeling
  • Deep molecular generative model
  • Self-supervised learning
